Basanti for Trade and Export is a company established in 2021. Its founders have 12 years of experience in the field of fishing and supplying freshwater crawfish in Egypt. We have five years of experience in processing and processing live and frozen freshwater lobster. The company is looking forward to exporting its live and frozen lobster products to North America, China and Europe

Temperatures

We always strive to apply ideal temperature standards in all stages of production. We maintain temperatures not less than 98 degrees Celsius while boiling products. We also maintain temperatures not exceeding 5 degrees Celsius during the process of cooling the product after boiling. We also maintain temperatures not exceeding 16 degrees Celsius in the production and packing halls. Our products are stored at temperatures of -18 throughout the storage and delivery period to our customers around the world.
